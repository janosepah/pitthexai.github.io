The Health + Explainable AI (HexAI) Research Laboratory at the University of Pittsburgh, led by Ahmad P. Tafti, has a simple goal: to make healthcare better through the power of explainable artificial intelligence (AI). Our research focuses on engineering, implementing, validating, and deploying cutting-edge fundamental and applied AI algorithms, and promote their applications in healthcare problems. We attempt to answer how advanced explainable AI strategies are developing in healthcare, how they shape healthcare systems, and how they impact the healthcare community.

The HexAI Research Laboratory is functioning in the Department of Health Information Managemnet at the School of Health and Rehabilitation Sciences. Our group in HexAI is a multidisciplinary team, and it benefits from several extramural collaborations with other academic institutions, nationwide and worldwide. Moreover, our group aims to provide highest quality education and research experience in AI-powered health informatics at all levels from K-12 to undergraduate to graduate and postdoctoral levels.

Ahmad P. Tafti, Ph.D.
Director, Pitt HexAI Lab

Research Interests

  • Explainable, interpretable, and accountable AI in healthcare.
  • AI biasness and reliability in healthcare.
  • AI-Powered healthcare for underserved and vulnerable population.
  • State-of-the-art deep learning methods for medical image analytics.
  • AI-assitive tool sets.
  • Open-source development to assist reproducible research in healthcare informatics.
